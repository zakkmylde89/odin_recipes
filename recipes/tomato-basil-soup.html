Tomato Basil Soup

Tomato Basil Soup is a great starter or even a great entree if served grilled cheese

Ingredients:

  • San Marzano Tomatoes: 1 #10 Can (6lbs 10oz)
  • Olive Oil: 4 Cups
  • Fresh Basil: 2 Whole Plants
  • Onion: 1 Diced
  • Garlic: 20 Cloves Crushed
  • Carrots: 6-8 Oven Charred
  • Shallot: 2 Whole
  • Italian Seasoning: 1/4 Cup
  • Red Wine: 1 Cup
  • Croutons

Process:

  • Put olive oil, 1 and half of the basil plants, shallot, and garlic into sauce pot on low heat
    and let cook for at least 1 hour. Remove from heat and let sit overnight.
  • Strain oil though shinwa or other mesh straining device
  • Rough chop carrots, coat in oil, and put in 425 degree oven until nicely charred
  • Place onions and carrots into a sauce pot on med-high heat and cook until onions are clear
  • Deglaze pan with red wine and let wine reduce for 5 minutes
  • Add San Marzano Tomatoes, strained oil, and Italian Seasoning to pan and cook for at least one hour
  • Use stick mixer to blend until everything is fully smooth
  • Top with remaining basil and croutons and serve
