Lemon Beurre Blanc

Lemon Beurre Blanc (or lemon white butter) is a citrus based cream sauce great for
many different applications. It pairs amazing with a light fish like Salmon or a vegetable
like asparagus.

Ingredients:

  • Butter: 1lb
  • Heavy Whipping Cream: 2qt
  • White Wine: 3 Cups
  • Lemons: 4 Peeled
  • Black Peppercorns: 1 TBLS
  • White Peppercorns: 1 tsp
  • Shallot: 2 Rough Chopped
  • Garlic: 8-10 Cloves Smashed
  • Green Onion: 2-3
  • Hot Sauce: 1/4 Cup
  • Worcestershire Sauce: 1/4 Cup
  • Red Pepper Flake: 1 TBLS
  • Bay Leaves: 3-5 Whole Leaves

Process:

  • Cut butter into small thin knobs and let set at room temp
  • Place all ingredients except butter and heavy cream into saucepan and
    cook on med-low heat until wine gels
  • Add heavy cream and reduce by half
  • Remove from heat and add butter 3-4 knobs at a time while constantly
    whisking until butter is fully melted and emulsified into cream
  • Run through shinwa strainer or other fine mesh device
  • Salt to taste and serve while still warm
